About Us

Welcome To Tandie health and wellness, where we focus on improving employee health

At Tylah Health & Wellness, our mission is to revolutionize occupational health services by providing care with a personal touch. We are committed to enhancing the physical and mental well-being of both employees and employers, ensuring a healthy and thriving work environment. With our nurse-led approach, we strive to create a nurturing and supportive atmosphere where everyone feels valued and supported in their journey towards wellness.

Our services

We offer a range of specialized services designed to enhance employee wellbeing, ensure safety, and promote a healthier workplace at competitive prices.
